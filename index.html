Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Fostering groundbreaking ideas, digital transformation, and technological advancement to shape a smarter, future-forward Bangladesh.

Sustainability

Promoting eco-friendly development, renewable energy, and conscious living to build a greener, resilient Bangladesh for generations.

Infrastructure

Developing smart cities, modern transport, and digital networks to support rapid, inclusive, and sustainable national growth.

Empowerment

Ensuring equal opportunities, quality education, and digital access to uplift every citizen and strengthen national progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

"I believe we can create a poverty-free world, because poverty is not created by poor people. It has been created and sustained by the economic and social systems we have designed for ourselves, the institutions and concepts that make up that system, and the policies that we pursue. If we want to end poverty, we must be willing to rethink those systems and redesign them with fairness and inclusion at their core. A world without poverty is not just a dream, it is a choice we can make through collective will and purposeful action."

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
